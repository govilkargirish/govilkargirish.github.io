    Legacy MSBI to Cloud Migration

    The Challenge:
    Your legacy SQL Server, SSIS, SSRS, and SSAS stack is:
    - Expensive to maintain and scale
    - Limiting your ability to innovate
    - Lacking modern capabilities (real-time, AI-ready)

    My Solution:
    Proven migration methodology that minimizes risk and downtime:

    1. Assessment - Inventory current state, dependencies, complexity
    2. Roadmap - Phased migration plan with clear milestones
    3. Quick Wins - Migrate high-impact, low-risk components first
    4. Validation - Parallel runs to ensure accuracy
    5. Cutover - Smooth transition with rollback plan
    6. Optimization - Post-migration performance tuning

    Typical Timeline: 3-6 months

    Pricing Philosophy

    Why I Don't Bill by the Hour

    Traditional hourly billing creates misaligned incentives:
    - You want efficiency; contractors get paid more for taking longer
    - You want strategic thinking; hourly billing rewards task execution
    - You want outcomes; hourly billing measures inputs

    My Approach: Value-Based Pricing

    I price based on:
    - The business value we're unlocking
    - The complexity of the challenge
    - The strategic importance to your organization

    This means:
    - Fixed-price projects with clear scope
    - Retainers with committed deliverables
    - Outcome-based success metrics

    Result: We're both incentivized toward the same goal‚Äîyour success.
